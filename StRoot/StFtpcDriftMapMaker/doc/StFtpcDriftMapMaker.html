FTPC Drift Map Maker 
  StFtpcDriftMapMaker 

StFtpcDriftMapMaker calculates the maps for electron drift in the
electric and magnetic field for the STAR Forward TPCs for the 
magnetic field (FF, HF, ZF, RHF or RF) and gas composition requested by the
FtpcDriftMapMaker.C macro.  The magnetic field is obtained using StarMagField.

The heart of the code are the algorithms of both magboltz1 and 2.

In the standard version, magboltz 1 is used because of the much shorter
calculation time.
