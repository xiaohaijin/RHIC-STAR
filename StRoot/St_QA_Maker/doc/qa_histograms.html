StRoot/St_QA_Maker histograms
  Maintained by K. Turner

Described in this Document:

  • The histograms of St_QA_Maker & StEventQAMaker

Methods in these classes:

  • St_QA_Maker - methods
    • Histograms are filled in these methods (using tables or StEvent): 

          MakeHistEvSum();  - event_summary
    MakeHistGlob();   - globtrk
    MakeHistPrim();   - dst_dedx
    MakeHistDE();     - primtrk
    MakeHistGen();    - particle
    MakeHistPID();    - primtrk,dst_dedx
    MakeHistVertex(); - dst_vertex,dst_xi_vertex,dst_v0_vertex,dst_kinkVertex
    MakeHistPoint();  - point
    MakeHistRich();   - g2t_rch_hit
    MakeHistEval();   - dst_vertex,g2t_vertex 
                        (g2t_vertex not on dst & only exists for simulated data)

Note : StEventQAMaker does not have access to Monte Carlo data, thus StEvent histograms for MakeHistGen, MakeHistRich, and MakeHistEval are empty.

Histograms:

  • MakeHistEvSum()
    • number of tracks (total, good, ratio pos. to neg., good primaries)
    • number of vertices, mean pT, mean eta, rms eta
    • x,y,z,r of primary vertex
  • MakeHistGlob()
    • number of tracks (total, good), iflag, detector id (good)
    • y vs x -dca of tracks to the beam axis,z-dca to beam axis
    • z-dca to beam axis vs tanl, vs z_first, vs psi, vs phi0
    • TPC, TPC+SVT, FTPC East & West histograms (1D) :
      - number of points, max. points, fit points on track
      - ratio of fit/tot, fit/max, charge
      - x,y,z,r position of first hit on track
      - track length, psi, eta, pT, momentum
      - chi squared, chi squared probability
      Not used for FTPC :
      - radius at start, azimuth at start (phi), z-coord. at start, log10 curvature
      - x,y,z of first hit minus x,y,z of helix at start, theta, tanl (dip angle)
      - overlayed histogram of first point: x,y,z_hit - x,y,z_helix
      - padrow # of first hit
      - impact param to primary vertex (log10 and not)
    • TPC, TPC+SVT, FTPC East and West histograms (2D) :
      - log pT vs eta, Y vs X of first hit on track
      - trk length vs eta, N pnts vs length, N fit pnts vs length
      Not used for FTPC :
      - tanl vs zfirst-zvtx, log mom vs trk length
      - chisq vs log mom, vs eta, vs phi, vs tanl, vs zfirst
      - chisq prob vs log mom, vs eta, vs tanl, vs zfirst
      - ratio Nfitpnt,Npnt vs log mom, vs Eta
      - psi vs phi
  • MakeHistPrim()
    • Note : uses essentially the same histograms as in MakeHistGlob(). Currently, FTPC histograms are empty for primary tracks and so not drawn (soon to change).
    • inner and outer sector TPC dE/dx histograms (drawn before event summary histograms)
  • MakeHistDE()
    • number of tracks
    • TPC,FTPC East & West histograms :
      - number of points to define dE/dx
      - dE/dx mean, sigma
  • MakeHistGen()
    • total num particles, num chg (e,mu,pi,K,p) part
    • charged pT (all & TPC only), charged eta, charged pT vs eta
    • generator prod vertex x,y,z
  • MakeHistPID()
    • p vs dedx (reconstructed)
  • MakeHistVertex()
    • number of vertices
    • Primary Vertex, Secondary Vertices histograms :
      - vertex id, x, y, z, r, chisq/dof
    • V0, Xi, Kink Vertices histograms :
      - number found, invariant mass (except for kinks)
  • MakeHistPoint
    • number of hits (total, tpc, svt, ssd, ftpc east & west)
    • detector id of hits (not filled for StEvent)
  • MakeHistRich()
    • multiplicity
  • MakeHistEval()
    • geant minus reconstructed x,y,z of primary vertex
    • reco primary vertex Z vs difference of geant and reco Z
